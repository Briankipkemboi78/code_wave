About Me

This is what I do.

Hello and welcome to my corner of the internet! I'm Brian Kipkemboi, an aspiring software engineer on a mission to explore the limitless possibilities of technology. With a background in data analysis and a burning passion for coding, I'm excited to share my story and aspirations with you.
